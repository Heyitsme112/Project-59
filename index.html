Why Harry Potter Is My Favorite Character

Harry Potter is my favorite character because I absolutely love the whole Harry Potter series!
I could say that I love all of the characters from the series, but I pick Harry Potter because he is the main character in the stories.
I like Harry Potter because of his loyal personality and bravery. His role throughout the stories is just exceptional!
He is always up to do anything for his friends even if it is risking his life against the evil.
Harry Potter is also a Gryfindor which mathces my house.
All in all, Harry Potter is my favorite character in the Harry Potter series.
